## My Journey

I'm a passionate AI engineer with a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning.
My Journey into AI began at age 15 with a simple yet profound question: <b>Can a machine think?</b>
Eight years later, I still don't have a definitive answer to that question and that uncertainty is part of what continues to motivate me.
Throughout my academic path, I've deepened my knowledge of deep learning, Natural Language Processing, Reinforcement Learning and hands-on experimentation.
Today my focus is on designing and building AI systems that can make a real-world impact. Through my work I aim to contribute step by step,
to pushing the field of Artificial Intelligence forward.
